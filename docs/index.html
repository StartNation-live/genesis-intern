Founders: Get help without hiring

Pilot program by StartNation

Most early-stage founders don’t need employees. They need support — without the cost, risk, or complexity of hiring.

We’re building a program that connects founders with AI-skilled student contributors through StartNation.

Typical setup

  • 5–10 hrs/week
  • remote
  • global
  • no hiring required

What's involved

Contributors support startups while gaining real startup experience using AI tools on real projects, through a compliant, unpaid internship model.

They can help with:

  • product testing, feedback & QA
  • user research & competitor analysis
  • AI-assisted content & growth experiments
  • lead generation & outreach
  • data collection & workflow testing
  • documentation & knowledge base setup

This is not traditional hiring — contributors are not employees or contractors of your company.

Contributors take part in a compliant internship designed to be:

  • lightweight for founders
  • focused on real-world experience

For founders, this means:

  • forward progress without hiring
  • fresh perspective
  • support without operational overhead
